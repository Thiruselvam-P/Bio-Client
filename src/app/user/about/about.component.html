Our Story

Where It All Began

MJ Fresh Bio was born from a deep love for the land and an even deeper concern for what ends up on your table. Founded in 2018, we started as a small collective of farmers and eco-enthusiasts who believed that sustainable agriculture was not just a choice — it was a responsibility.

Today, we source directly from trusted organic farms across India, bringing you the freshest kitchenware, millets, drinkware, and eco-friendly tableware — all without a single synthetic chemical in sight. Every product carries our promise: pure origins, transparent practices, and love for the planet.

Our Vision
